Harvey Milk Day Kick-Off

📍 West Hollywood Park Friday, May 22, 6:00 PM

The City of West Hollywood marks Harvey Milk Day with the 4th annual José Julio Sarria Drag Pageant. Harvey Milk was the first openly LGBTQ+ person elected to public office in California. The evening opens WeHo Pride season.

WeHo Pride Arts Festival

📍 The Village at Ed Gould Plaza and other venues Saturday–Sunday, May 23–24

California Primary Election

📍 Statewide Ballots mail May 4. Registration deadline May 18.

Under California's top-two primary, the two highest finishers advance to November regardless of party. With Republicans Hilton and Bianco currently leading the field, Democratic turnout matters. Make a plan to vote: by mail, drop box, or in person.

WeHo Pride Street Fair

📍 Santa Monica Boulevard Saturday–Sunday, June 6–7

A free two-day street fair along Santa Monica Boulevard with exhibitors, two community stages, and live performances. Other Saturday events along the boulevard include the Women's Freedom Festival and the Dyke March. The OUTLOUD music festival runs all weekend at West Hollywood Park (separate ticketing).
