Morning Coffee & Planning Meeting

📍 The Doheny Room West Hollywood Aquatic and Recreation Center, 8750 El Tovar Place

A chance for WeHo Indivisible members and neighbors to get to know each other, share what's on our minds, and start planning through the midterms.

Parking: If you're driving, the easiest parking is the public garage at San Vicente and Melrose, right next to the park. The first hour is free, and you can get an additional hour with validation at the ARC front desk on the second floor.

Walking in: If you're coming through West Hollywood Park, head to the grand staircase up to the Aquatic and Recreation Center, then to the second floor, where the Doheny Room is located.

May Day Strong — Workers Over Billionaires

📍 Events across the region Find one near you

May Day Strong is a national day of action organized by a coalition including Indivisible, the NEA, National Nurses United, MoveOn, and others. The theme is Workers Over Billionaires. Participants are asked to refuse business as usual: no work, no school, no shopping.

Rallies and marches in dozens of locations across greater Los Angeles and beyond.
