Ways to Participate

Pick Your Level

Not everyone has the same amount of time or energy — and that's fine. These are three real ways to plug in, from the smallest to the most involved.

01

Show Up

The lowest-lift option — and genuinely valuable. Presence is power, and showing up tells your neighbors they're not in this alone.

  • Attend a rally or march
  • Come to a community meeting
  • Sign and share a petition
  • Post about an action online
  • Bring a friend to an event
See Upcoming Events
02

Take Action

Five to twenty minutes, from your phone or computer. Constituent calls and emails are among the most effective tools we have.

  • Call your representative (5 min)
  • Email Congress using our scripts
  • Join a group phone bank
  • Write a letter to the editor
  • Sign up for action alerts

03

Help Organize

We're a small group and we want to grow. Every skill is useful — design, logistics, research, outreach, or just showing up reliably.

  • Help plan and run events
  • Handle outreach or social media
  • Research bills and local issues
  • Take on a recurring role
  • Help build our leadership team
